About

My name is aaron and I would like to briefly tell you about the impact fitness has had on my life. I use to just work and play video games and I had no guidance in life. I started excercising every since I graduated high school and It has gave me greater meaning. I started having more energy and motivation. I gained the confidence to have more social interactions and ended up meeting my wife and building a family.

Excercise gave me the motivation to get back into school, I earned my associates in accounting and now work a well paying government job. I am now back in school to follow my passion in technology. Taking care of my health gives me the energy to study, work and chase my two sons around the park. I have created this website to help share the knowledge of the fitness with the hope these tips will Assist you in fulfilling your dreams.
