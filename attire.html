Attire

  • Running shoes - Having the right shoes is important before you begin physical activity. Shoes come with different comfort and material. Athletic shoes are using labeled by their cushion, stabilty, and weight. Some people enjoy their shoes firm, some like them extra soft, the choice is yours.
  • Comfortable Clothing - Clothing can make or break workout. Having proper clothes such as Dri-fit and loose clothing with help your performance. Be sure to dress according to the weather conditions to avoid getting sick.

  • Music and headphones - This is not required but it can make your workout even better. Some thrive off of the beats and lyrics of their favorite song, give music a try and see if it helps you push through that last set of push ups.
